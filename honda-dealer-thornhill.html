Driving to Maple Honda from Thornhill

Maple Honda is in the Vaughan auto cluster at 89 Auto Vaughan Drive, near Major Mackenzie and Jane. Most Thornhill addresses reach it in 15 to 25 minutes depending on time of day.

From Yonge & Steeles

North on Yonge to Major Mackenzie, west to Jane, then north into the auto cluster. Or take Highway 7 west from Yonge and turn north at Jane.

From Bathurst & Centre

North on Bathurst to Major Mackenzie, then west. A flat, well-signed route that bypasses Highway 7 traffic.

From Dufferin south of Steeles

North on Dufferin to Major Mackenzie, then a short westbound run. Often the fastest evening route from west Thornhill.

FAQ from Thornhill buyers

Is Maple Honda the closest Honda dealer to Thornhill?

Not always — there are Honda stores closer to south Thornhill on the Toronto side. But "closest" rarely decides where a Honda shopper buys. Most decide based on price, inventory, and salesperson fit, which is the case Henry is trying to make.

Can I do most of the back-and-forth over text?

Yes. Henry expects that. Most Thornhill buyers handle pricing, trade, and trim selection by text or email and only come in for the test drive and signing.

What about a Honda lease coming off in the next 90 days?

That is a great window. Henry can lay out buy-out vs. re-lease vs. switch-to-new before your lease end so you do not get stuck reacting in the last week.

Do you handle pre-owned Toyota or Lexus too?

Yes. Pre-owned Toyota, Lexus, Mazda, and other brands come through Maple Honda regularly. The pre-owned search form on the main site is the fastest way to put a request in front of Henry.

What if I am still six months out from buying?

That is a normal Thornhill timeline. Henry will not push a same-day decision. Use the comparison guides on the main site to keep narrowing, and reach out when you are 30-60 days out.
