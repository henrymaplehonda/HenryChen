2026 HONDA ODYSSEY

Feature highlights by trim level. Each trim below builds on the previous one.

Book a Test Drive

SPORT

Base trim / starting point

USB Ports & Power Outlets

  • 1 USB-A port and 1 USB-C port in 1st row
  • 2 USB-C ports in 2nd row
  • 2 USB-C ports in 3rd row
  • 115V front power outlet
  • 12-volt power outlets (1x front, 1x cargo)

Seating & Interior

  • 8-passenger seating with Magic Slide 2nd-row seats and one-motion 60/40 split 3rd-row Magic Seat
  • Heated driver's seat with 8-way power adjustment and 4-way power lumbar support
  • Heated front passenger's seat with 4-way power adjustment
  • Heated leather-wrapped steering wheel
  • Red ambient lighting
  • Seatbelt reminder for all seating positions
  • Seatback pocket (1st-row and upper pockets)
  • Integrated 2nd-row sunshades
  • Tri-zone automatic climate control with air-filtration system

Infotainment & Connectivity

  • 9" touchscreen centre display
  • Advanced rear entertainment system with 12.8" high-resolution screen, HDMI interface, USB-A (console compartment), and headphone jack (3rd row)
  • Wireless Apple CarPlay / Android Auto
  • CabinTalk in-car PA
  • 7 speakers including subwoofer

Safety Features

  • Blind Spot Information (BSI) system with Cross Traffic Monitor (CTM)
  • Traffic Sign Recognition (TSR)
  • Adaptive Cruise Control (ACC) with low-speed follow (LSF)
  • Forward Collision Warning (FCW) system
  • Collision Mitigation Braking System (CMBS)
  • Lane Departure Warning (LDW) system
  • Lane Keeping Assist System (LKAS)
  • Road Departure Mitigation (RDM) system
  • Multi-angle rearview camera with dynamic guidelines

Exterior Features

  • 18" aluminum-alloy wheels - painted black
  • LED fog lights, headlights (auto-on/off), taillights, and daytime running lights
  • One-touch power moonroof with tilt feature
  • Power sliding doors
  • Auto high beam
  • Body-coloured door handles
  • Idle-stop

Other Features

  • HandsFreeLink bilingual Bluetooth
  • Proximity key entry system with push-button start
  • Remote engine start
  • Electronic parking brake (EPB) with automatic brake hold
  • Maintenance Minder system
  • Conversation mirror with sunglasses holder
  • Active shutter grille
  • Lower anchors and tethers for children (LATCH) (5)
  • Tire Pressure Monitoring System (TPMS) with tire fill assist

  • Acoustic windshield
  • Auto-dimming rearview mirror

  • Heated leather steering wheel with red stitching
  • HomeLink remote system
  • Integrated 2nd- and 3rd-row sunshades
  • Leather-trimmed seating surfaces (front and outboard 2nd row)
  • Memory-linked side mirrors with reverse gear tilt-down
  • Power tailgate with programmable height
  • Seatback pocket (1st-row and upper pockets, 2nd-row)
  • SiriusXM
  • Wireless charging
