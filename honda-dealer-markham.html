Driving to Maple Honda from Markham

Maple Honda is in the Vaughan auto cluster at 89 Auto Vaughan Drive, near Major Mackenzie and Jane. Most Markham addresses reach it in 20 to 30 minutes outside of rush hour.

From Highway 407

Westbound 407 to Highway 400 north, exit at Major Mackenzie, east, then north into the auto cluster. The most predictable route during weekday traffic.

From Highway 7 (Unionville)

Westbound on Highway 7 the whole way through Richmond Hill and into Vaughan, then north at Jane. A good evening route.

From Markham Road / Kennedy

West on 16th Avenue or Major Mackenzie. Slightly longer but avoids the Highway 7 corridor at peak times.

FAQ from Markham buyers

Is Maple Honda the closest Honda to Markham?

No — there are Honda stores closer to central Markham. The Maple Honda case is not "closest" — it is "second-quote worth getting" and "salesperson worth keeping." Many Markham buyers end up here because they want both.

Can I do everything remotely until the test drive?

Yes. Pricing, trade-in starting number, trim and colour confirmation, finance or lease structure all happen by text or email. You drive over once.

What about my Lexus / BMW / Audi trade-in?

Common Markham trade. Send year, mileage, exterior and interior photos. Henry can come back with a starting number quickly and finalize on-site.

Do you handle Honda lease returns from a different rooftop?

Yes. A Honda lease originated at any Canadian Honda rooftop can be returned, bought out, or rolled into a new vehicle through Maple Honda. Henry can lay out all three options before your end date.

I am still 6 months out — too early?

Not at all. The earlier you talk, the better the timing on incentives, pre-orders, and trade-in cycle. Use the comparison guides and feature sheets on the main site to keep narrowing.
