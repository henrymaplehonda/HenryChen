2026 HONDA PILOT

Feature highlights by trim level. Each trim below builds on the one before it.

Book a Test Drive

SPORT

Base trim / starting point

  • 20" shark grey alloy wheels
  • Black roof rails
  • LED headlights, DRLs, and fog lights
  • Heated power side mirrors
  • Power tailgate with programmable height
  • Rear privacy glass + rear camera washer
  • 8-passenger seating
  • Heated front seats + heated leather-wrapped steering wheel
  • Tri-zone climate control
  • 10.2" digital cluster + 12.3" touchscreen
  • Wireless Apple CarPlay® / Android Auto™
  • 9-speaker audio with subwoofer
  • Wireless charging + remote engine starter
  • HondaLink® Assist + proximity key

Safety & Driver Assist

  • Adaptive Cruise Control with Low-Speed Follow
  • Collision Mitigation Braking System™
  • Forward Collision Warning
  • Lane Keeping Assist + Lane Departure Warning
  • Road Departure Mitigation
  • Traffic Jam Assist + Traffic Sign Recognition
  • Blind Spot Information + Rear Cross Traffic Monitor
  • Auto High Beam + Driver Attention Monitor
  • 8-airbag system

Performance

  • 3.5L V6: 285 hp / 262 lb-ft
  • 10-speed automatic transmission
  • i-VTM4 AWD
  • Paddle shifters
  • Independent multi-link rear suspension
  • Electronic parking brake + brake hold
  • Idle-stop + capless fueling system

EX-L

Additional features than Sport

  • 18" pewter grey alloy wheels
  • Acoustic windshield
  • Front and rear parking sensors
  • Leather-trimmed 1st-row seats
  • Leatherette 2nd-row seats + heated outboard seats
  • Integrated 2nd-row sunshades
  • One-touch 2nd row with removable center seat
  • In-trunk seat storage
  • Auto-dimming rearview mirror
  • Conversation mirror
  • 3rd-row USB-C ports (2)
  • 2nd-row climate controls
  • Low-speed braking control
  • Memory-linked mirrors with reverse tilt-down
  • Driver memory seat
  • Extra drive modes: Tow, Sport, Trail, Sand

TRAILSPORT

Additional features than EX-L

  • 7-passenger seating
  • 18" shark grey TrailSport wheels
  • All-terrain tires + full-size spare
  • Heated 2nd-row captain's chairs
  • Panoramic moonroof
  • 110V outlet in 2nd row
  • HomeLink® remote system
  • Multi-view 360-degree camera
  • Higher-torque rear differential with Trail Torque Logic
  • Steel front skid plate
  • Class III trailer hitch with 7-pin connector
  • TrailSport suspension tuning
  • Increased ground clearance
  • Improved approach and breakover angles
  • Gloss black door handles
  • Orange contrast stitching + embroidered headrests

TOURING

Additional features than TrailSport

  • 20" machined-finish shark grey wheels
  • Acoustic laminated front door glass
  • LED puddle lights
  • Front camera washer
  • Perforated leather-trimmed seating
  • Ventilated front seats
  • Head-Up Display
  • 2nd-row ambient lighting
  • Bose® premium audio with 12 speakers
  • Hands-free power tailgate with walk-away close
  • Rain-sensing wipers
  • Smart Entry for front/rear doors and tailgate
  • One-touch 7- or 8-passenger seating
  • In-trunk seat storage

Touring swaps out some TrailSport off-road items like the high-torque rear differential, steel skid plate, and built-in trailer hitch.

BLACK EDITION

Additional features than Touring

  • 20" Berlina Black alloy wheels
  • Black Edition exterior accents
  • Perforated black leather seats with Black Edition logo
  • Black Edition stitching and floor mats
  • Red ambient interior lighting
