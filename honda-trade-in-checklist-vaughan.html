Honda Trade-In Checklist (Vaughan)

Use this checklist before your appraisal so your trade-in process is faster and cleaner.

Documents to bring

  • Driver’s license
  • Ownership/registration
  • Loan payout info (if financed)
  • Service records (if available)

Vehicle prep (30 minutes)

  • Remove personal items
  • Quick wash/vacuum
  • Fix inexpensive items (burnt bulbs, low washer fluid)
  • Bring both key fobs

What affects value most

  • Condition (interior/exterior)
  • Kilometres and maintenance history
  • Accident history
  • Market demand for your model/trim
