Honda Buying FAQ (Vaughan)

Quick answers to the most common questions I get from Honda buyers in Vaughan and the GTA.

1) Lease or finance?

If you value flexibility and lower monthly payments, leasing is usually better. If you keep vehicles longer and want to own the car, financing is usually better.

2) How long does delivery take?

It depends on model, trim, and color. In-stock units can be quick; factory orders can take longer. I always provide realistic timelines before you commit.

3) Can I trade in my current vehicle?

Yes. We appraise your vehicle and apply the value to your next deal. If you still have a loan, we calculate the payout and show your exact equity position.

4) What should I bring to save time?

Bring your driver’s license, proof of insurance, and (if trading in) ownership/registration details. This helps speed up approvals and paperwork.
